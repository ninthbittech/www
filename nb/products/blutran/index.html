BluTran

BluTran provides a highly portable solution to enable file sending as well as receiving over wireless networks using Bluetooth technology. Due to its versatility as well as ease of use, it is most effective in carrying out mass advertisements, application transfers and file transfers easily at areas having high footfalls like malls, banks, offices, restaurants and other social-gathering locations. Due to its multi-platform deployment capability, it can be easily and quickly deployed on existing hardware setups as well as on dedicated standalone hardware at minimal costs.
